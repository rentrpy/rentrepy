Ren / Rentrepy

Hello! I'm a self-taught artist from the Philippines who loves to draw cute things while sipping on coffee. I've been drawing and taking commissions since 2021. My goal is to share my journey and inspire others while gaining recognition along the way.

This time, I've decided to experiment and explore a bunch in multimedia.
